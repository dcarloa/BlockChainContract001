Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What's the difference between Simple Mode and Blockchain Mode?

+

Simple Mode: No crypto wallet needed. Sign in with Google/Email. Track expenses in 12 currencies, get Smart Settlements, completely free. Perfect for trips, events, shared apartments. Available now!

Blockchain Mode: Coming Soon! Will use smart contracts for automatic execution. On-chain voting, immutable records. Perfect for DAOs and crypto-native groups. Currently in development.

Do I need a crypto wallet to use AntPool?

+

No! Simply sign in with Google or Email—no wallet required. Blockchain Mode (which will require MetaMask or Base Wallet) is currently in development and coming soon.

What currencies can I use in Simple Mode?

+

We support 12 currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, MXN, COP, BRL, CAD, AUD, JPY, CNY, INR, CHF. Track expenses in multiple currencies within the same group. Perfect for international trips or global teams.

How does Smart Settlements work?

+

Our algorithm optimizes who pays whom to minimize total transactions. Example: If Alice owes Bob $50 and Bob owes Charlie $50, instead of 2 payments, Alice pays Charlie directly. Maximum efficiency, just like ant colonies!

How much does it cost to use AntPool?

+

Simple Mode: Completely free forever. No subscriptions, no commissions, no hidden fees.

Blockchain Mode: Only blockchain gas fees (~$0.01 per transaction on Base L2). Creating a group costs ~$1, deposits/votes ~$0.01 each.

Is it secure? Can my money be stolen?

+

Simple Mode: Your data is stored in Firebase with industry-standard encryption. Only group members can see expenses.

Blockchain Mode: Funds protected by audited smart contracts. Only group members can vote/execute. No one, not even us, can access your money without approval.

Can I use AntPool on my phone?

+

Yes! AntPool is mobile-first. Compact cards, touch-optimized buttons, responsive layout. Works perfectly on iOS, Android, and desktop browsers.

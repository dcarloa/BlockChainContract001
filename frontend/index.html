Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need technical knowledge to use SplitExpense?

+

No. You only need a wallet like MetaMask or Base Wallet. The process is as simple as using any mobile app. We guide you through every step.

How much does it cost to use SplitExpense?

+

There are no subscriptions or commissions. You only pay blockchain gas (~$0.01 per transaction on Base). Creating a group costs ~$1, depositing ~$0.01.

Is it secure? Can my money be stolen?

+

Funds are protected by audited smart contracts. Only group members can vote and execute expenses. No one, not even us, can access your money without your consent.

What happens if a member disagrees with an expense?

+

Everyone can vote on each proposal. 60% approval is required. If the proposal doesn't reach the threshold, it's not executed and the money remains in the common fund.

Can I withdraw my money at any time?

+

Yes. The group creator can remove you by returning your proportional share. Or at the end of the project, you can settle your positive balance. Your money is always yours.

What blockchain do you use?

+

We use Base, an Ethereum Layer 2. It's ultra-fast (2 seconds), super cheap (~$0.01/tx), and backed by Coinbase. Compatible with all Ethereum wallets.

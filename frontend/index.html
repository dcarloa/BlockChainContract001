Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I start?

+

Sign in with Google or Email. Create a group in 30 seconds. Invite your colony. Done.

What currencies work?

+

12 currencies: USD, EUR, MXN, COP, BRL, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, CNY, INR, CHF. Perfect for international trips.

How do settlements work?

+

We calculate who owes who with the fewest payments possible. You settle however you want (cash, transfer, etc).

Is it really free?

+

Yes. Free tier for unlimited expense tracking. No ads, no tricks.

Is my data safe?

+

Protected by Firebase security. Only your colony members see your data. We don't sell information.

Can I use it on my phone?

+

Absolutely. Mobile-first design. Works on any device. Add it to your home screen like an app.

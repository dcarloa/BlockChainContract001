DESCRIPTION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ant Pool is a collaborative expense tracking and organization application
designed to help groups manage shared expenses efficiently.

The service enables users to:
- Create expense-sharing groups
- Track shared expenses and balances
- Calculate expense distributions automatically
- Invite members and collaborate on expense management
- Organize group finances with transparency

IMPORTANT NOTICES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
⚠️ FREE SERVICE:
   Ant Pool currently operates as a completely free service. Any future paid

⚠️ NO PAYMENT PROCESSING:
